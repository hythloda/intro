Apply for Featured App status on Canton Network.

Featured App status helps strong applications stand out in the ecosystem and gives the Foundation and the network a clearer way to review readiness, scale, and ongoing compliance.

If you want your application to be considered, start with the request form below. After that, review the Featured App guidance, the coupon guidance, and the locking FAQ so your team knows the current expectations.

Why Featured Apps matter

  • They make strong applications easier for users, partners, and ecosystem participants to discover.
  • They help the Foundation prioritize apps that are operationally ready for growth and network participation.
  • They create clearer expectations around PartyID usage, scaling behavior, issuer classification, and compliance.
  • They help align application review with tokenomics, accountability, and production-readiness requirements.

Featured App Rules

  • Review the Featured App Coupon Guidance.
  • Review the Featured App Locking FAQ.
  • Maximum of 2,500 new parties onboarded per week.
  • You will be added to the Slack channel #ext-temp-party-scaling.
  • UTXO management should target merging at 10 UTXOs, with less than or equal to 4 per party on average.
  • Airdrop drip campaigns must remain below 0.125 TPS per app.
  • High frequency billing should not exceed 1 transaction per customer every 12 hours.
